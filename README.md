# Superstore Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project analyses sales performance for **Superstore X** using **PostgreSQL, SQL, Power BI, and DAX**.

The objective was to evaluate business performance across multiple dimensions including:
- Sales trends 
- Regional performance 
- Customer segment performance 
- Product category performance 

The project identifies key business insights and provides strategic recommendations to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Business Objectives
- Analyse overall sales performance 
- Identify high-performing products 
- Evaluate regional sales performance 
- Examine customer segment contribution 
- Generate actionable business recommendations 

---

## Tools & Technologies Used
- **PostgreSQL** – Database management 
- **SQL** – Data extraction and analysis 
- **Power BI** – Dashboard development and visualisation 
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – KPI and calculated measures

## Dashboard Overview
The Power BI dashboard provides an interactive overview of Superstore X sales performance.

### KPI Metrics
- **Total Sales:** $2.26M 
- **Total Orders:** 5K 
- **Total Customers:** 793 
- **Average Order Value:** $459.48 

Dashboard visuals include:
- Sales by Year 
- Sales by Region 
- Sales by Customer Segment 
- Sales by Category 
- Sales by Sub-Category 

---

## Key Insights

### Sales Trends
- Total sales increased from **$479K in 2015** to **$722K in 2018**
- Sales declined by approximately **4.3% between 2015–2016**
- Strong growth was observed in 2017 and 2018 

### Regional Performance
- **West** was the highest-performing region, generating **$710K (31%)** of total sales 
- **South** was the lowest-performing region with **$389K** in sales 

### Customer Segment Analysis
- **Consumer** generated **$1.15M**, accounting for **50.9%** of total sales 
- **Home Office** generated the lowest sales contribution 

### Product Performance
- **Phones** generated the highest revenue at **$330K** 
- **Chairs** generated **$323K** 
- Combined, these represented approximately **29% of total company sales** 

---

## Recommendations
- Increase investment in high-performing products such as Phones and Chairs 
- Investigate South region underperformance 
- Implement targeted strategies to improve Home Office and Corporate sales performance
